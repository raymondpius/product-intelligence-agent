End-to-End Workflow

How the Agent Works

From a PM's question to a specific, validated prescription — completely autonomous.

01
Initiation
PM Submits a Business Question
The PM inputs a high-level product concern — churn spikes, activation drops, expansion bottlenecks. The Orchestrator parses intent and initializes the agent workflow.
INPUT → "Why are new users on our Pro plan churning at 2x the rate of Team plan users in month 1?"
02
Diagnosis
Hypothesis Engine Explores the Data
The Hypothesis Discovery Engine seeds broad cohorts, validates each against live metrics via the Data Engineer Agent, scores signal strength, and prunes low-value paths using Beam Search.
DISCOVERY → Pro users, Tenure < 30d, zero_integrations_connected = TRUE → churn rate 18.3% vs 4.1% baseline
03
Deep Dive
Principal Analyst Synthesizes Signals
The analyst agent enriches the high-signal cohort with behavioral data: session patterns, feature adoption, in-app events, support contacts — building a full narrative around the cohort.
SIGNAL → Cohort averages 1.2 sessions in week 1 (vs 6.8 for retained). 0% used core value feature. 40% opened no emails.
04
Prescription
Actionable Strategy Generated
The Orchestrator compiles findings into a final report with specific, prioritized product and go-to-market recommendations tied directly to the validated cohort data.
PRESCRIPTION → Launch targeted onboarding flow for Pro users with zero integrations at day 3. Predicted churn reduction: ~40%.
